Research Publications

Elsevier · Published

Physics-Informed Residual Learning for Sensorless Torque Estimation in PMSM Drives

A hybrid physics-informed deep learning framework for real-time sensorless torque estimation in Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor drives. Combines domain knowledge with residual neural networks, significantly reducing dependency on physical sensors while maintaining high estimation accuracy under dynamic load conditions.

Physics-Informed AI Motor Drives Industrial Deployment Deep Learning
02

Applied Energy (Target) · Under Review

Renewable Energy Penetration and Electricity Market Dynamics in Germany and Spain

A comprehensive econometric and machine learning analysis of renewable energy integration effects on electricity market pricing in Germany and Spain. Leverages 87,000+ hourly observations with XGBoost, SHAP-based interpretability, and calibrated uncertainty quantification to deliver policy-relevant insights.

Renewable Energy XGBoost + SHAP 87K+ Observations Uncertainty Quantification
03

Ongoing Research

Calibration-Aware Solar PV Fault Detection using Trustworthy Machine Learning

A calibration-aware fault detection framework for solar PV systems that prioritizes prediction reliability alongside accuracy. Employs temperature scaling, conformal prediction, and ensemble uncertainty estimation to produce trustworthy fault alerts suitable for safety-critical operational environments.
